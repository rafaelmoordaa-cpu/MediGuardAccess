# MediGuardAccess Hypertension Learning E-Book

Panduan edukatif hipertensi untuk aplikasi MediGuardAccess. Isi: pengertian hipertensi, tanda awal, cara ukur tekanan darah, Smart BP Recheck, pencegahan, red flag, dan doctor note.

## Rujukan Inti
- WHO Hypertension fact sheet.
- International Society of Hypertension lifestyle management position paper 2023.
- 2023 ESH Guidelines for arterial hypertension.
- 2024 ESC Guidelines for elevated blood pressure and hypertension.
- 2025 AHA/ACC high blood pressure guideline.
- Kemenkes RI: Kenali dan Kendalikan Hipertensi; CERDIK; PATUH.
